# Google Scholar Upgrade Guide

## Introduction
This guide outlines the necessary steps to effectively upgrade your Google Scholar profile, specifically for researcher Sana Begum. This includes detailing current publications and the research focus for optimal visibility.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Log in to Your Google Scholar Account
1. Go to [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com).
2. Click on the "Sign in" button located at the top right corner.
3. Enter your email and password associated with your Google Scholar account.

### Step 2: Review Your Current Profile
1. Once logged in, click on "My Profile" at the top.
2. Review the existing information, including your name, affiliations, and photo.

### Step 3: Update Personal Information
1. Click on the edit button (pencil icon) near your name.
2. Ensure your name is correctly spelled and up to date.
3. Add or update your current affiliation if necessary.
4. Upload a recent professional photograph (if not already uploaded).

### Step 4: Add or Update Publications
1. In the "My Profile" section, below your name, you will see a list of your contributions.
2. To add a new publication:
   - Click on the "+" icon.
   - Choose the appropriate option to add a publication (e.g., "Add article manually" or "Import from PubMed").
   - Provide accurate details including title, authors, journal name, and year.

3. To update existing publications:
   - Locate the specific entry.
   - Click on the edit button (pencil icon) beside the publication.
   - Make necessary changes and click "Save".

### Step 5: Highlight Your Research Focus
1. Click on the “Edit” button (pencil icon) near the “Research Interests” section.
2. Clearly describe your main research areas and interests. For example: 
   - **Current Research Focus:** 
     - Bioinformatics
     - Computational Biology
     - Data Science in Life Sciences
3. Use relevant keywords that reflect your research for better discoverability.

### Step 6: Set Up Alerts for New Publications
1. In your profile, navigate to the “Alerts” section.
2. Set up alerts based on keywords relevant to your research.
3. This will notify you when new publications in your field are published.

### Step 7: Share Your Profile
1. Once your profile is updated, share your Google Scholar profile link:
   - Click on “View profile” and copy the URL.
   - Share this link on your personal website, LinkedIn, and other relevant platforms.

## Conclusion
Regularly updating your Google Scholar profile is crucial for maintaining visibility in your field. Ensure that you follow these steps every few months or whenever you publish new works to keep your profile current.
