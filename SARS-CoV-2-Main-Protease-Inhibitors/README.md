# SARS-CoV-2 Main Protease Inhibitor Discovery

## Introduction
This project focuses on the discovery of potential inhibitors targeting the Main Protease (MPro) of SARS-CoV-2, a key enzyme essential for viral replication.

## Research Methodology
Our research methodology consists of the following steps:
1. **Literature Review**: Comprehensive study of existing research on SARS-CoV-2 MPro and known inhibitors.
2. **Molecular Docking**: Utilization of software tools to simulate the binding of potential inhibitors with MPro.
3. **Virtual Screening**: Screening of compound libraries to identify molecules with high binding affinity.

## Workflow
The workflow can be divided into the following major phases:
1. **Data Collection**: Gathering relevant data on SARS-CoV-2 MPro structure and potential inhibitors.
2. **Simulation and Modelling**: Use of computational techniques to model interactions between MPro and inhibitors.
3. **Analysis of Results**: Evaluation of docking scores and visual inspections of binding conformations.

## Analysis Approach
- **Binding Affinity Calculation**: Assessing the binding strength of inhibitors using docking scores.
- **Comparative Analysis**: Comparing results with known inhibitors to validate findings.
- **Visualization Tools**: Utilizing molecular visualization software to present the binding interactions effectively.
